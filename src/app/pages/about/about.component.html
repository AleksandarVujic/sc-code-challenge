SmartCat Pharmacy

This application is an admin page designed for the efficient management of medicament products. It features three primary sections:

  • Statistics Page: Displays insightful data visualizations using pie charts and bar charts. The pie chart shows the distribution of products by manufacturers, while the bar chart represents product quantities with their prices on the y-axis.
  • Product Management Page: Allows users to perform CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations on the product catalog, ensuring streamlined management of medicament inventory.
  • About Page: Provides a brief overview of the application and its author, highlighting its purpose and features.
